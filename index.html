About Us

Avery Dennison (NYSE: AVY) is a global company with locations in over 50 countries, and we employ approximately 36,000 people worldwide with the head office in Glendale, California. We provide branding and information labeling solutions, including pressure-sensitive materials, radio-frequency identification (RFID) inlays and tags, and a variety of converted products and solutions. We design and manufacture a wide variety of labeling and functional materials that enhance branded packaging, carry or display information that connects the physical and the digital and improve customers’ product performance.

Since our entry in 2003, Avery Dennison has been building a strong presence in Vietnam. Our flagship Long An plant provides world class manufacturing facilities together with distribution centers located in Ho Chi Minh city & Bac Ninh. The modern facilities incorporate Avery Dennison’s global materials science and know-how capabilities in packaging and labeling solutions, helping our Vietnamese customers seamlessly do business in Vietnam as well as integrate into the global supply chain.

Commercial Apprentice Program 2024 (English & Chinese/Japanese speaking)

The Commercial Apprentice Program 2024 (CAP 2024) recruitment program is organized by Avery Dennison Vietnam, with a special focus on potential candidates who are final-year students or have less than 2 years of work experience. Proficiency in both English and Chinese/Japanese is a requirement, aimed at developing and training young talents with the goal of progressing to the role of Commercial Executive within just 12 - 18 months.

Recruitment Requirements:

  • Bachelor's/Master's degree holders who have completed their academic programs or have a maximum of 2 years of full-time experience, available for full-time employment from March 2024.
  • Proficiency in Chinese (equivelant to HSK5 level) or have HSK5 certificate.
  • Proficiency in English (equivelant to IELTS 6.0 level) or have IELTS 6.0 certificate.
  • Having a strong business acumen and a passion for business activities.
  • Effective communication skill at all levels of the organization and strong adaptability/ Flexible working style in both individual or team collaboration.
  • Willing to relocate to the assigned work location (HCMC, HN) based on business requirements is a strong plus.

Application period: 29/12/2023 - 31/01/2024
