Commercial Apprentice Program 2024

The Commercial Apprentice Program 2024 (CAP 2024) recruitment program is organized by Avery Dennison Vietnam, with a special focus on potential candidates who are final-year students or have less than 2 years of work experience. Proficiency in both English and Chinese is a requirement, aimed at developing and training young talents with the goal of progressing to the role of Commercial Executive within just 12 months.

Recruitment Requirements:

  • Bachelor's/Master's degree holders who have completed their academic programs or have a maximum of 2 years of experience, available for full-time employment in 2024.
  • Proficiency in Chinese at HSK5 level.
  • Proficiency in English at IELTS 6.0 level.
  • Possess analytical skills and a passion for business activities.
  • Ability to work in a team, adaptability, and effective communication skills.

Application period: 29/12/2023 - 31/01/2024
